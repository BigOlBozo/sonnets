b'<div class="div1" id="Son-001">\n<div class="page sceneHeader bold">1</div>\n<br/><a name="Son-001-01"></a>From fairest creatures we desire increase,<br/><a name="Son-001-02"></a>That thereby beauty's rose might never die,<br/><a name="Son-001-03"></a>But, as the riper should by time decease,<br/><a name="Son-001-04"></a>His tender heir might bear his memory.<br/><a name="Son-001-05"></a>But thou, contracted to thine own bright eyes,<br/><a name="Son-001-06"></a>Feed'st thy light's flame with self-substantial fuel,<br/><a name="Son-001-07"></a>Making a famine where abundance lies,<br/><a name="Son-001-08"></a>Thyself thy foe, to thy sweet self too cruel.<br/><a name="Son-001-09"></a>Thou that art now the world's fresh ornament<br/><a name="Son-001-10"></a>And only herald to the gaudy spring<br/><a name="Son-001-11"></a>Within thine own bud buriest thy content<br/><a name="Son-001-12"></a>And, tender churl, mak'st waste in niggarding.<br/><a name="Son-001-13"></a>Pity the world, or else this glutton be-<br/><a name="Son-001-14"></a>To eat the world's due, by the grave and thee.</div>'
