<br/><a name="Son-136-01"></a>If thy soul check thee that I come so near,<br/><a name="Son-136-02"></a>Swear to thy blind soul that I was thy will,<br/><a name="Son-136-03"></a>And will, thy soul knows, is admitted there.<br/><a name="Son-136-04"></a><span class="lineNbr" name="Son-136-04">4</span>Thus far for love my love-suit, sweet, fulfill.<br/><a name="Son-136-05"></a>Will will fulfill the treasure of thy love,<br/><a name="Son-136-06"></a>Ay, fill it full with wills, and my will one.<br/><a name="Son-136-07"></a>In things of great receipt with ease we prove<br/><a name="Son-136-08"></a><span class="lineNbr" name="Son-136-08">8</span>Among a number one is reckoned none.<br/><a name="Son-136-09"></a>Then in the number let me pass untold,<br/><a name="Son-136-10"></a>Though in thy store’s account I one must be.<br/><a name="Son-136-11"></a>For nothing hold me, so it please thee hold<br/><a name="Son-136-12"></a><span class="lineNbr" name="Son-136-12">12</span>That nothing me, a something, sweet, to thee.<br/><a name="Son-136-13"></a><span class="alignment" style="padding-left:10px;"> </span>Make but my name thy love, and love that still,<br/><a name="Son-136-14"></a><span class="alignment" style="padding-left:10px;"> </span>And then thou lovest me, for my name is Will.</div>
