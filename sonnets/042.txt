b'<div class="div1" id="Son-042">\n<div class="page sceneHeader bold">42</div>\n<br/><a name="Son-042-01"></a>That thou hast her, it is not all my grief,<br/><a name="Son-042-02"></a>And yet it may be said I loved her dearly;<br/><a name="Son-042-03"></a>That she hath thee is of my wailing chief,<br/><a name="Son-042-04"></a>A loss in love that touches me more nearly.<br/><a name="Son-042-05"></a>Loving offenders, thus I will excuse ye:<br/><a name="Son-042-06"></a>Thou dost love her because thou know'st I love her,<br/><a name="Son-042-07"></a>And for my sake even so doth she abuse me,<br/><a name="Son-042-08"></a><span class="lineNbr" name="Son-042-08">8</span>Suff'ring my friend for my sake to approve her.<br/><a name="Son-042-09"></a>If I lose thee, my loss is my love's gain,<br/><a name="Son-042-10"></a>And losing her, my friend hath found that loss;<br/><a name="Son-042-11"></a>Both find each other, and span class="lineNbr" name="Son-042-12">12</span>And both for my sake lay on me this cross.<br/><a name="Son-042-13"></a><span class="alignment" style="padding-left:10px;">\xc2\xa0</span>But here's the joy: my friend and I are one;<br/><a name="Son-042-14"></a><span class="alignment" style="padding-left:10px;">\xc2\xa0</span>Sweet flattery! then she loves but me alone.</div>'
