<br/><a name="Son-030-01"></a>When to the sessions of sweet silent thought<br/><a name="Son-030-02"></a>I summon up remembrance of things past,<br/><a name="Son-030-03"></a>I sigh the lack of many a thing I sought,<br/><a name="Son-030-04"></a><span class="lineNbr" name="Son-030-04">4</span>And with old woes new wail my dear time’s waste;<br/><a name="Son-030-05"></a>Then can I drown an eye, unused to flow,<br/><a name="Son-030-06"></a>For precious friends hid in death’s dateless night,<br/><a name="Son-030-07"></a>And weep afresh love’s long since canceled woe,<br/><a name="Son-030-08"></a><span class="lineNbr" name="Son-030-08">8</span>And moan th’ expense of many a vanished sight.<br/><a name="Son-030-09"></a>Then can I grieve at grievances foregone,<br/><a name="Son-030-10"></a>And heavily from woe to woe tell o’er<br/><a name="Son-030-11"></a>The sad account of fore-bemoanèd moan,<br/><a name="Son-030-12"></a><span class="lineNbr" name="Son-030-12">12</span>Which I new pay as if not paid before.<br/><a name="Son-030-13"></a><span class="alignment" style="padding-left:10px;"> </span>But if the while I think on thee, dear friend,<br/><a name="Son-030-14"></a><span class="alignment" style="padding-left:10px;"> </span>All losses are restored and sorrows end.</div>
