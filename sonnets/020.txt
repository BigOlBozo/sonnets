<br/><a name="Son-020-01"></a>A woman’s face with Nature’s own hand painted<br/><a name="Son-020-02"></a>Hast thou, the master mistress of my passion;<br/><a name="Son-020-03"></a>A woman’s gentle heart, but not acquainted<br/><a name="Son-020-04"></a><span class="lineNbr" name="Son-020-04">4</span>With shifting change, as is false women’s fashion;<br/><a name="Son-020-05"></a>An eye more bright than theirs, less false in rolling,<br/><a name="Son-020-06"></a>Gilding the object whereupon it gazeth;<br/><a name="Son-020-07"></a>A man in hue all hues in his controlling,<br/><a name="Son-020-08"></a><span class="lineNbr" name="Son-020-08">8</span>Which steals men’s eyes and women’s souls amazeth.<br/><a name="Son-020-09"></a>And for a woman wert thou first created,<br/><a name="Son-020-10"></a>Till Nature as she wrought thee fell a-doting,<br/><a name="Son-020-11"></a>And by addition me of thee defeated<br/><a name="Son-020-12"></a><span class="lineNbr" name="Son-020-12">12</span>By adding one thing to my purpose nothing.<br/><a name="Son-020-13"></a><span class="alignment" style="padding-left:10px;"> </span>But since she pricked thee out for women’s pleasure,<br/><a name="Son-020-14"></a><span class="alignment" style="padding-left:10px;"> </span>Mine be thy love, and thy love’s use their treasure.</div>
