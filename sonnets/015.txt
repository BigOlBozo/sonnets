<br/><a name="Son-015-01"></a>When I consider everything that grows<br/><a name="Son-015-02"></a>Holds in perfection but a little moment,<br/><a name="Son-015-03"></a>That this huge stage presenteth nought but shows<br/><a name="Son-015-04"></a><span class="lineNbr" name="Son-015-04">4</span>Whereon the stars in secret influence comment;<br/><a name="Son-015-05"></a>When I perceive that men as plants increase,<br/><a name="Son-015-06"></a>Cheerèd and checked even by the selfsame sky,<br/><a name="Son-015-07"></a>Vaunt in their youthful sap, at height decrease,<br/><a name="Son-015-08"></a><span class="lineNbr" name="Son-015-08">8</span>And wear their brave state out of memory;<br/><a name="Son-015-09"></a>Then the conceit of this inconstant stay<br/><a name="Son-015-10"></a>Sets you most rich in youth before my sight,<br/><a name="Son-015-11"></a>Where wasteful Time debateth with Decay<br/><a name="Son-015-12"></a><span class="lineNbr" name="Son-015-12">12</span>To change your day of youth to sullied night;<br/><a name="Son-015-13"></a><span class="alignment" style="padding-left:10px;"> </span>And, all in war with Time for love of you,<br/><a name="Son-015-14"></a><span class="alignment" style="padding-left:10px;"> </span>As he takes from you, I engraft you new.</div>
