b'<div class="div1" id="Son-076">\n<div class="page sceneHeader bold">76</div>\n<br/><a name="Son-076-01"></a>Why is my verse so barren of new pride,<br/><a name="Son-076-02"></a>So far from variation or quick change?<br/><a name="Son-076-03"></a>Why with the time do I not glance aside<br/><a name="Son-076-04"></a><span class="lineNbr" name="Son-076-04">4</span>To new-found methods and to compounds strange?<br/><a name="Son-076-05"></a>Why write I still all one, ever the same,<br/><a name="Son-076-06"></a>And keep invention in a noted weed,<br/><a name="Son-076-07"></a>That every word doth almost <span class="half-square">\xe2\x8c\x9c</span>tell<span class="half-square">\xe2\x8c\x9d</span> my name,<br/><a name="Son-076-08"></a><span class="lineNbr" name="Son-076-08">8</span>Showing their birth and where they did proceed?<br/><a name="Son-076-09"></a>O, know, sweet love, I always write of you,<br/><a name="Son-076-10"></a>And you and love are still my argument;<br/><a name="Son-076-11"></a>So all my best is dressing old words new,<br/><a name="Son-076-12"></a><span class="lineNbr" name="Son-076-12">12</span>Spending again what is already spent.<br/><a name="Son-076-13"></a><span class="alignment" style="padding-left:10px;">\xc2\xa0</span>For as the sun is daily new and old,<br/><a name="Son-076-14"></a><span class="alignment" style="padding-left:10px;">\xc2\xa0</span>So is my love, still telling what is told.</div>'
