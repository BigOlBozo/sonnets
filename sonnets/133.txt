<div class="page sceneHeader bold">133</div>
<br/><a name="Son-133-01"></a>Beshrew that heart that makes my heart to groan<br/><a name="Son-133-02"></a>For that deep wound it gives my friend and me.<br/><a name="Son-133-03"></a>Is ’t not enough to torture me alone,<br/><a name="Son-133-04"></a><span class="lineNbr" name="Son-133-04">4</span>But slave to slavery my sweet’st friend must be?<br/><a name="Son-133-05"></a>Me from myself thy cruel eye hath taken,<br/><a name="Son-133-06"></a>And my next self thou harder hast engrossed;<br/><a name="Son-133-07"></a>Of him, myself, and thee I am forsaken,<br/><a name="Son-133-08"></a><span class="lineNbr" name="Son-133-08">8</span>A torment thrice threefold thus to be crossed.<br/><a name="Son-133-09"></a>Prison my heart in thy steel bosom’s ward,<br/><a name="Son-133-10"></a>But then my friend’s heart let my poor heart bail.<br/><a name="Son-133-11"></a>Whoe’er keeps me, let my heart be his guard;<br/><a name="Son-133-12"></a><span class="lineNbr" name="Son-133-12">12</span>Thou canst not then use rigor in my jail.<br/><a name="Son-133-13"></a><span class="alignment" style="padding-left:10px;"> </span>And yet thou wilt, for I, being pent in thee,<br/><a name="Son-133-14"></a><span class="alignment" style="padding-left:10px;"> </span>Perforce am thine, and all that is in me.</div>
