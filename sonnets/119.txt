<div class="page sceneHeader bold">119</div>
<br/><a name="Son-119-01"></a>What potions have I drunk of siren tears<br/><a name="Son-119-02"></a>Distilled from limbecks foul as hell within,<br/><a name="Son-119-03"></a>Applying fears to hopes and hopes to fears,<br/><a name="Son-119-04"></a><span class="lineNbr" name="Son-119-04">4</span>Still losing when I saw myself to win!<br/><a name="Son-119-05"></a>What wretched errors hath my heart committed,<br/><a name="Son-119-06"></a>Whilst it hath thought itself so blessèd never!<br/><a name="Son-119-07"></a>How have mine eyes out of their spheres been fitted<br/><a name="Son-119-08"></a><span class="lineNbr" name="Son-119-08">8</span>In the distraction of this madding fever!<br/><a name="Son-119-09"></a>O, benefit of ill! Now I find true<br/><a name="Son-119-10"></a>That better is by evil still made better;<br/><a name="Son-119-11"></a>And ruined love, when it is built anew,<br/><a name="Son-119-12"></a><span class="lineNbr" name="Son-119-12">12</span>Grows fairer than at first, more strong, far greater.<br/><a name="Son-119-13"></a><span class="alignment" style="padding-left:10px;"> </span>So I return rebuked to my content,<br/><a name="Son-119-14"></a><span class="alignment" style="padding-left:10px;"> </span>And gain by ills thrice more than I have spent.</div>
