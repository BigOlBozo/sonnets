<div class="page sceneHeader bold">116</div>
<br/><a name="Son-116-01"></a>Let me not to the marriage of true minds<br/><a name="Son-116-02"></a>Admit impediments. Love is not love<br/><a name="Son-116-03"></a>Which alters when it alteration finds<br/><a name="Son-116-04"></a><span class="lineNbr" name="Son-116-04">4</span>Or bends with the remover to remove.<br/><a name="Son-116-05"></a>O, no, it is an ever-fixèd mark<br/><a name="Son-116-06"></a>That looks on tempests and is never shaken;<br/><a name="Son-116-07"></a>It is the star to every wand’ring bark,<br/><a name="Son-116-08"></a><span class="lineNbr" name="Son-116-08">8</span>Whose worth’s unknown, although his height be taken.<br/><a name="Son-116-09"></a>Love’s not Time’s fool, though rosy lips and cheeks<br/><a name="Son-116-10"></a>Within his bending sickle’s compass come;<br/><a name="Son-116-11"></a>Love alters not with his brief hours and weeks,<br/><a name="Son-116-12"></a><span class="lineNbr" name="Son-116-12">12</span>But bears it out even to the edge of doom.<br/><a name="Son-116-13"></a><span class="alignment" style="padding-left:10px;"> </span>If this be error, and upon me proved,<br/><a name="Son-116-14"></a><span class="alignment" style="padding-left:10px;"> </span>I never writ, nor no man ever loved.</div>
