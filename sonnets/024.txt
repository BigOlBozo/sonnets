<br/><a name="Son-024-01"></a>Mine eye hath played the painter and hath <span class="half-square">⌜</span>stelled<span class="half-square">⌝</span><br/><a name="Son-024-02"></a>Thy beauty’s form in table of my heart;<br/><a name="Son-024-03"></a>My body is the frame wherein ’tis held,<br/><a name="Son-024-04"></a><span class="lineNbr" name="Son-024-04">4</span>And perspective it is best painter’s art.<br/><a name="Son-024-05"></a>For through the painter must you see his skill<br/><a name="Son-024-06"></a>To find where your true image pictured lies,<br/><a name="Son-024-07"></a>Which in my bosom’s shop is hanging still,<br/><a name="Son-024-08"></a><span class="lineNbr" name="Son-024-08">8</span>That hath his windows glazèd with thine eyes.<br/><a name="Son-024-09"></a>Now see what good turns eyes for eyes have done:<br/><a name="Son-024-10"></a>Mine eyes have drawn thy shape, and thine for me<br/><a name="Son-024-11"></a>Are windows to my breast, wherethrough the sun<br/><a name="Son-024-12"></a><span class="lineNbr" name="Son-024-12">12</span>Delights to peep, to gaze therein on thee.<br/><a name="Son-024-13"></a><span class="alignment" style="padding-left:10px;"> </span>Yet eyes this cunning want to grace their art:<br/><a name="Son-024-14"></a><span class="alignment" style="padding-left:10px;"> </span>They draw but what they see, know not the heart.</div>
