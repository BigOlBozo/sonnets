<div class="page sceneHeader bold">121</div>
<br/><a name="Son-121-01"></a>’Tis better to be vile than vile esteemed,<br/><a name="Son-121-02"></a>When not to be receives reproach of being,<br/><a name="Son-121-03"></a>And the just pleasure lost, which is so deemed<br/><a name="Son-121-04"></a><span class="lineNbr" name="Son-121-04">4</span>Not by our feeling but by others’ seeing.<br/><a name="Son-121-05"></a>For why should others’ false adulterate eyes<br/><a name="Son-121-06"></a>Give salutation to my sportive blood?<br/><a name="Son-121-07"></a>Or on my frailties why are frailer spies,<br/><a name="Son-121-08"></a><span class="lineNbr" name="Son-121-08">8</span>Which in their wills count bad what I think good?<br/><a name="Son-121-09"></a>No, I am that I am; and they that level<br/><a name="Son-121-10"></a>At my abuses reckon up their own.<br/><a name="Son-121-11"></a>I may be straight though they themselves be bevel;<br/><a name="Son-121-12"></a><span class="lineNbr" name="Son-121-12">12</span>By their rank thoughts my deeds must not be shown,<br/><a name="Son-121-13"></a><span class="alignment" style="padding-left:10px;"> </span>Unless this general evil they maintain:<br/><a name="Son-121-14"></a><span class="alignment" style="padding-left:10px;"> </span>All men are bad and in their badness reign.</div>
