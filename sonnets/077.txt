<br/><a name="Son-077-01"></a>Thy glass will show thee how thy beauties wear,<br/><a name="Son-077-02"></a>Thy dial how thy precious minutes waste;<br/><a name="Son-077-03"></a>The vacant leaves thy mind’s imprint will bear,<br/><a name="Son-077-04"></a><span class="lineNbr" name="Son-077-04">4</span>And of this book this learning mayst thou taste:<br/><a name="Son-077-05"></a>The wrinkles which thy glass will truly show,<br/><a name="Son-077-06"></a>Of mouthèd graves will give thee memory;<br/><a name="Son-077-07"></a>Thou by thy dial’s shady stealth mayst know<br/><a name="Son-077-08"></a><span class="lineNbr" name="Son-077-08">8</span>Time’s thievish progress to eternity.<br/><a name="Son-077-09"></a>Look what thy memory cannot contain<br/><a name="Son-077-10"></a>Commit to these waste <span class="half-square">⌜</span>blanks,<span class="half-square">⌝</span> and thou shalt find<br/><a name="Son-077-11"></a>Those children nursed, delivered from thy brain,<br/><a name="Son-077-12"></a><span class="lineNbr" name="Son-077-12">12</span>To take a new acquaintance of thy mind.<br/><a name="Son-077-13"></a><span class="alignment" style="padding-left:10px;"> </span>These offices, so oft as thou wilt look,<br/><a name="Son-077-14"></a><span class="alignment" style="padding-left:10px;"> </span>Shall profit thee and much enrich thy book.</div>
