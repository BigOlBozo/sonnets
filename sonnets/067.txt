<br/><a name="Son-067-01"></a>Ah, wherefore with infection should he live,<br/><a name="Son-067-02"></a>And with his presence grace impiety,<br/><a name="Son-067-03"></a>That sin by him advantage should achieve<br/><a name="Son-067-04"></a><span class="lineNbr" name="Son-067-04">4</span>And lace itself with his society?<br/><a name="Son-067-05"></a>Why should false painting imitate his cheek<br/><a name="Son-067-06"></a>And steal dead seeing of his living hue?<br/><a name="Son-067-07"></a>Why should poor beauty indirectly seek<br/><a name="Son-067-08"></a><span class="lineNbr" name="Son-067-08">8</span>Roses of shadow, since his rose is true?<br/><a name="Son-067-09"></a>Why should he live, now Nature bankrout is,<br/><a name="Son-067-10"></a>Beggared of blood to blush through lively veins,<br/><a name="Son-067-11"></a>For she hath no exchequer now but his,<br/><a name="Son-067-12"></a><span class="lineNbr" name="Son-067-12">12</span>And, proud of many, lives upon his gains?<br/><a name="Son-067-13"></a><span class="alignment" style="padding-left:10px;"> </span>O, him she stores, to show what wealth she had<br/><a name="Son-067-14"></a><span class="alignment" style="padding-left:10px;"> </span>In days long since, before these last so bad.</div>
