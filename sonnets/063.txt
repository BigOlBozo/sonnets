<br/><a name="Son-063-01"></a>Against my love shall be, as I am now,<br/><a name="Son-063-02"></a>With Time’s injurious hand crushed and o’erworn;<br/><a name="Son-063-03"></a>When hours have drained his blood and filled his brow<br/><a name="Son-063-04"></a><span class="lineNbr" name="Son-063-04">4</span>With lines and wrinkles; when his youthful morn<br/><a name="Son-063-05"></a>Hath traveled on to age’s steepy night,<br/><a name="Son-063-06"></a>And all those beauties whereof now he’s king<br/><a name="Son-063-07"></a>Are vanishing, or vanished out of sight,<br/><a name="Son-063-08"></a><span class="lineNbr" name="Son-063-08">8</span>Stealing away the treasure of his spring;<br/><a name="Son-063-09"></a>For such a time do I now fortify<br/><a name="Son-063-10"></a>Against confounding age’s cruel knife,<br/><a name="Son-063-11"></a>That he shall never cut from memory<br/><a name="Son-063-12"></a><span class="lineNbr" name="Son-063-12">12</span>My sweet love’s beauty, though my lover’s life.<br/><a name="Son-063-13"></a><span class="alignment" style="padding-left:10px;"> </span>His beauty shall in these black lines be seen,<br/><a name="Son-063-14"></a><span class="alignment" style="padding-left:10px;"> </span>And they shall live, and he in them still green.</div>
