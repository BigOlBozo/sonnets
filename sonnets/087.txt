<br/><a name="Son-087-01"></a>Farewell, thou art too dear for my possessing,<br/><a name="Son-087-02"></a>And like enough thou know’st thy estimate.<br/><a name="Son-087-03"></a>The charter of thy worth gives thee releasing;<br/><a name="Son-087-04"></a><span class="lineNbr" name="Son-087-04">4</span>My bonds in thee are all determinate.<br/><a name="Son-087-05"></a>For how do I hold thee but by thy granting,<br/><a name="Son-087-06"></a>And for that riches where is my deserving?<br/><a name="Son-087-07"></a>The cause of this fair gift in me is wanting,<br/><a name="Son-087-08"></a><span class="lineNbr" name="Son-087-08">8</span>And so my patent back again is swerving.<br/><a name="Son-087-09"></a>Thy self thou gav’st, thy own worth then not knowing,<br/><a name="Son-087-10"></a>Or me, to whom thou gav’st it, else mistaking;<br/><a name="Son-087-11"></a>So thy great gift, upon misprision growing,<br/><a name="Son-087-12"></a><span class="lineNbr" name="Son-087-12">12</span>Comes home again, on better judgment making.<br/><a name="Son-087-13"></a><span class="alignment" style="padding-left:10px;"> </span>Thus have I had thee as a dream doth flatter,<br/><a name="Son-087-14"></a><span class="alignment" style="padding-left:10px;"> </span>In sleep a king, but waking no such matter.</div>
