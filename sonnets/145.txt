<br/><a name="Son-145-01"></a>Those lips that Love’s own hand did make<br/><a name="Son-145-02"></a>Breathed forth the sound that said “I hate”<br/><a name="Son-145-03"></a>To me that languished for her sake;<br/><a name="Son-145-04"></a><span class="lineNbr" name="Son-145-04">4</span>But when she saw my woeful state,<br/><a name="Son-145-05"></a>Straight in her heart did mercy come,<br/><a name="Son-145-06"></a>Chiding that tongue that ever sweet<br/><a name="Son-145-07"></a>Was used in giving gentle doom,<br/><a name="Son-145-08"></a><span class="lineNbr" name="Son-145-08">8</span>And taught it thus anew to greet:<br/><a name="Son-145-09"></a>“I hate” she altered with an end<br/><a name="Son-145-10"></a>That followed it as gentle day<br/><a name="Son-145-11"></a>Doth follow night, who, like a fiend,<br/><a name="Son-145-12"></a><span class="lineNbr" name="Son-145-12">12</span>From heaven to hell is flown away.<br/><a name="Son-145-13"></a><span class="alignment" style="padding-left:10px;"> </span>“I hate” from hate away she threw,<br/><a name="Son-145-14"></a><span class="alignment" style="padding-left:10px;"> </span>And saved my life, saying “not you.”</div>
