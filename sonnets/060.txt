<br/><a name="Son-060-01"></a>Like as the waves make towards the pebbled shore,<br/><a name="Son-060-02"></a>So do our minutes hasten to their end,<br/><a name="Son-060-03"></a>Each changing place with that which goes before;<br/><a name="Son-060-04"></a><span class="lineNbr" name="Son-060-04">4</span>In sequent toil all forwards do contend.<br/><a name="Son-060-05"></a>Nativity, once in the main of light,<br/><a name="Son-060-06"></a>Crawls to maturity, wherewith being crowned,<br/><a name="Son-060-07"></a>Crookèd eclipses ’gainst his glory fight,<br/><a name="Son-060-08"></a><span class="lineNbr" name="Son-060-08">8</span>And Time that gave doth now his gift confound.<br/><a name="Son-060-09"></a>Time doth transfix the flourish set on youth<br/><a name="Son-060-10"></a>And delves the parallels in beauty’s brow,<br/><a name="Son-060-11"></a>Feeds on the rarities of Nature’s truth,<br/><a name="Son-060-12"></a><span class="lineNbr" name="Son-060-12">12</span>And nothing stands but for his scythe to mow.<br/><a name="Son-060-13"></a><span class="alignment" style="padding-left:10px;"> </span>And yet to times in hope my verse shall stand,<br/><a name="Son-060-14"></a><span class="alignment" style="padding-left:10px;"> </span>Praising thy worth, despite his cruel hand.</div>
