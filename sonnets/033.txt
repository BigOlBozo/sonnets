<br/><a name="Son-033-01"></a>Full many a glorious morning have I seen<br/><a name="Son-033-02"></a>Flatter the mountain tops with sovereign eye,<br/><a name="Son-033-03"></a>Kissing with golden face the meadows green,<br/><a name="Son-033-04"></a><span class="lineNbr" name="Son-033-04">4</span>Gilding pale streams with heavenly alchemy,<br/><a name="Son-033-05"></a>Anon permit the basest clouds to ride<br/><a name="Son-033-06"></a>With ugly rack on his celestial face,<br/><a name="Son-033-07"></a>And from the forlorn world his visage hide,<br/><a name="Son-033-08"></a><span class="lineNbr" name="Son-033-08">8</span>Stealing unseen to west with this disgrace.<br/><a name="Son-033-09"></a>Even so my sun one early morn did shine<br/><a name="Son-033-10"></a>With all-triumphant splendor on my brow,<br/><a name="Son-033-11"></a>But, out alack, he was but one hour mine;<br/><a name="Son-033-12"></a><span class="lineNbr" name="Son-033-12">12</span>The region cloud hath masked him from me now.<br/><a name="Son-033-13"></a><span class="alignment" style="padding-left:10px;"> </span>Yet him for this my love no whit disdaineth;<br/><a name="Son-033-14"></a><span class="alignment" style="padding-left:10px;"> </span>Suns of the world may stain when heaven’s sun staineth.</div>
