<br/><a name="Son-146-01"></a>Poor soul, the center of my sinful earth,<br/><a name="Son-146-02"></a><span class="half-square">⌜</span>Pressed with<span class="half-square">⌝</span> these rebel powers that thee array,<br/><a name="Son-146-03"></a>Why dost thou pine within and suffer dearth,<br/><a name="Son-146-04"></a><span class="lineNbr" name="Son-146-04">4</span>Painting thy outward walls so costly gay?<br/><a name="Son-146-05"></a>Why so large cost, having so short a lease,<br/><a name="Son-146-06"></a>Dost thou upon thy fading mansion spend?<br/><a name="Son-146-07"></a>Shall worms, inheritors of this excess,<br/><a name="Son-146-08"></a><span class="lineNbr" name="Son-146-08">8</span>Eat up thy charge? Is this thy body’s end?<br/><a name="Son-146-09"></a>Then, soul, live thou upon thy servant’s loss,<br/><a name="Son-146-10"></a>And let that pine to aggravate thy store.<br/><a name="Son-146-11"></a>Buy terms divine in selling hours of dross;<br/><a name="Son-146-12"></a><span class="lineNbr" name="Son-146-12">12</span>Within be fed, without be rich no more.<br/><a name="Son-146-13"></a><span class="alignment" style="padding-left:10px;"> </span>So shalt thou feed on Death, that feeds on men,<br/><a name="Son-146-14"></a><span class="alignment" style="padding-left:10px;"> </span>And Death once dead, there’s no more dying then.</div>
