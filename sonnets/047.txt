<br/><a name="Son-047-01"></a>Betwixt mine eye and heart a league is took,<br/><a name="Son-047-02"></a>And each doth good turns now unto the other.<br/><a name="Son-047-03"></a>When that mine eye is famished for a look,<br/><a name="Son-047-04"></a><span class="lineNbr" name="Son-047-04">4</span>Or heart in love with sighs himself doth smother,<br/><a name="Son-047-05"></a>With my love’s picture then my eye doth feast<br/><a name="Son-047-06"></a>And to the painted banquet bids my heart.<br/><a name="Son-047-07"></a>Another time mine eye is my heart’s guest<br/><a name="Son-047-08"></a><span class="lineNbr" name="Son-047-08">8</span>And in his thoughts of love doth share a part.<br/><a name="Son-047-09"></a>So, either by thy picture or my love,<br/><a name="Son-047-10"></a>Thyself away are present still with me;<br/><a name="Son-047-11"></a>For thou <span class="half-square">⌜</span>no<span class="half-square">⌝</span> farther than my thoughts canst move,<br/><a name="Son-047-12"></a><span class="lineNbr" name="Son-047-12">12</span>And I am still with them, and they with thee;<br/><a name="Son-047-13"></a><span class="alignment" style="padding-left:10px;"> </span>Or, if they sleep, thy picture in my sight<br/><a name="Son-047-14"></a><span class="alignment" style="padding-left:10px;"> </span>Awakes my heart to heart’s and eye’s delight.</div>
