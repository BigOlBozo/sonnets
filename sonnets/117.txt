<br/><a name="Son-117-01"></a>Accuse me thus: that I have scanted all<br/><a name="Son-117-02"></a>Wherein I should your great deserts repay,<br/><a name="Son-117-03"></a>Forgot upon your dearest love to call,<br/><a name="Son-117-04"></a><span class="lineNbr" name="Son-117-04">4</span>Whereto all bonds do tie me day by day;<br/><a name="Son-117-05"></a>That I have frequent been with unknown minds,<br/><a name="Son-117-06"></a>And given to time your own dear-purchased right;<br/><a name="Son-117-07"></a>That I have hoisted sail to all the winds<br/><a name="Son-117-08"></a><span class="lineNbr" name="Son-117-08">8</span>Which should transport me farthest from your sight.<br/><a name="Son-117-09"></a>Book both my willfulness and errors down,<br/><a name="Son-117-10"></a>And on just proof surmise accumulate;<br/><a name="Son-117-11"></a>Bring me within the level of your frown,<br/><a name="Son-117-12"></a><span class="lineNbr" name="Son-117-12">12</span>But shoot not at me in your wakened hate,<br/><a name="Son-117-13"></a><span class="alignment" style="padding-left:10px;"> </span>Since my appeal says I did strive to prove<br/><a name="Son-117-14"></a><span class="alignment" style="padding-left:10px;"> </span>The constancy and virtue of your love.</div>
