<br/><a name="Son-036-01"></a>Let me confess that we two must be twain<br/><a name="Son-036-02"></a>Although our undivided loves are one;<br/><a name="Son-036-03"></a>So shall those blots that do with me remain,<br/><a name="Son-036-04"></a><span class="lineNbr" name="Son-036-04">4</span>Without thy help, by me be borne alone.<br/><a name="Son-036-05"></a>In our two loves there is but one respect,<br/><a name="Son-036-06"></a>Though in our lives a separable spite,<br/><a name="Son-036-07"></a>Which though it alter not love’s sole effect,<br/><a name="Son-036-08"></a><span class="lineNbr" name="Son-036-08">8</span>Yet doth it steal sweet hours from love’s delight.<br/><a name="Son-036-09"></a>I may not evermore acknowledge thee,<br/><a name="Son-036-10"></a>Lest my bewailèd guilt should do thee shame,<br/><a name="Son-036-11"></a>Nor thou with public kindness honor me<br/><a name="Son-036-12"></a><span class="lineNbr" name="Son-036-12">12</span>Unless thou take that honor from thy name.<br/><a name="Son-036-13"></a><span class="alignment" style="padding-left:10px;"> </span>But do not so. I love thee in such sort<br/><a name="Son-036-14"></a><span class="alignment" style="padding-left:10px;"> </span>As, thou being mine, mine is thy good report.</div>
