<br/><a name="Son-052-01"></a>So am I as the rich whose blessèd key<br/><a name="Son-052-02"></a>Can bring him to his sweet up-lockèd treasure,<br/><a name="Son-052-03"></a>The which he will not ev’ry hour survey,<br/><a name="Son-052-04"></a><span class="lineNbr" name="Son-052-04">4</span>For blunting the fine point of seldom pleasure.<br/><a name="Son-052-05"></a>Therefore are feasts so solemn and so rare,<br/><a name="Son-052-06"></a>Since seldom coming in the long year set,<br/><a name="Son-052-07"></a>Like stones of worth they thinly placèd are,<br/><a name="Son-052-08"></a><span class="lineNbr" name="Son-052-08">8</span>Or captain jewels in the carcanet.<br/><a name="Son-052-09"></a>So is the time that keeps you as my chest,<br/><a name="Son-052-10"></a>Or as the wardrobe which the robe doth hide<br/><a name="Son-052-11"></a>To make some special instant special blessed<br/><a name="Son-052-12"></a><span class="lineNbr" name="Son-052-12">12</span>By new unfolding his imprisoned pride.<br/><a name="Son-052-13"></a><span class="alignment" style="padding-left:10px;"> </span>Blessèd are you whose worthiness gives scope,<br/><a name="Son-052-14"></a><span class="alignment" style="padding-left:10px;"> </span>Being had, to triumph, being lacked, to hope.</div>
