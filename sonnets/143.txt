<div class="page sceneHeader bold">143</div>
<br/><a name="Son-143-01"></a>Lo, as a careful huswife runs to catch<br/><a name="Son-143-02"></a>One of her feathered creatures broke away,<br/><a name="Son-143-03"></a>Sets down her babe, and makes all swift dispatch<br/><a name="Son-143-04"></a><span class="lineNbr" name="Son-143-04">4</span>In pursuit of the thing she would have stay,<br/><a name="Son-143-05"></a>Whilst her neglected child holds her in chase,<br/><a name="Son-143-06"></a>Cries to catch her whose busy care is bent<br/><a name="Son-143-07"></a>To follow that which flies before her face,<br/><a name="Son-143-08"></a><span class="lineNbr" name="Son-143-08">8</span>Not prizing her poor infant’s discontent;<br/><a name="Son-143-09"></a>So runn’st thou after that which flies from thee,<br/><a name="Son-143-10"></a>Whilst I, thy babe, chase thee afar behind.<br/><a name="Son-143-11"></a>But if thou catch thy hope, turn back to me<br/><a name="Son-143-12"></a><span class="lineNbr" name="Son-143-12">12</span>And play the mother’s part: kiss me, be kind.<br/><a name="Son-143-13"></a><span class="alignment" style="padding-left:10px;"> </span>So will I pray that thou mayst have thy will,<br/><a name="Son-143-14"></a><span class="alignment" style="padding-left:10px;"> </span>If thou turn back and my loud crying still.</div>
