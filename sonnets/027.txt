<br/><a name="Son-027-01"></a>Weary with toil, I haste me to my bed,<br/><a name="Son-027-02"></a>The dear repose for limbs with travel tired,<br/><a name="Son-027-03"></a>But then begins a journey in my head<br/><a name="Son-027-04"></a><span class="lineNbr" name="Son-027-04">4</span>To work my mind when body’s work’s expired.<br/><a name="Son-027-05"></a>For then my thoughts, from far where I abide,<br/><a name="Son-027-06"></a>Intend a zealous pilgrimage to thee,<br/><a name="Son-027-07"></a>And keep my drooping eyelids open wide,<br/><a name="Son-027-08"></a><span class="lineNbr" name="Son-027-08">8</span>Looking on darkness which the blind do see;<br/><a name="Son-027-09"></a>Save that my soul’s imaginary sight<br/><a name="Son-027-10"></a>Presents <span class="half-square">⌜</span>thy<span class="half-square">⌝</span> shadow to my sightless view,<br/><a name="Son-027-11"></a>Which like a jewel hung in ghastly night<br/><a name="Son-027-12"></a><span class="lineNbr" name="Son-027-12">12</span>Makes black night beauteous and her old face new.<br/><a name="Son-027-13"></a><span class="alignment" style="padding-left:10px;"> </span>Lo, thus, by day my limbs, by night my mind,<br/><a name="Son-027-14"></a><span class="alignment" style="padding-left:10px;"> </span>For thee and for myself no quiet find.</div>
