<div class="page sceneHeader bold">138</div>
<br/><a name="Son-138-01"></a>When my love swears that she is made of truth<br/><a name="Son-138-02"></a>I do believe her though I know she lies,<br/><a name="Son-138-03"></a>That she might think me some untutored youth,<br/><a name="Son-138-04"></a><span class="lineNbr" name="Son-138-04">4</span>Unlearnèd in the world’s false subtleties.<br/><a name="Son-138-05"></a>Thus vainly thinking that she thinks me young,<br/><a name="Son-138-06"></a>Although she knows my days are past the best,<br/><a name="Son-138-07"></a>Simply I credit her false-speaking tongue;<br/><a name="Son-138-08"></a><span class="lineNbr" name="Son-138-08">8</span>On both sides thus is simple truth suppressed.<br/><a name="Son-138-09"></a>But wherefore says she not she is unjust?<br/><a name="Son-138-10"></a>And wherefore say not I that I am old?<br/><a name="Son-138-11"></a>O, love’s best habit is in seeming trust,<br/><a name="Son-138-12"></a><span class="lineNbr" name="Son-138-12">12</span>And age in love loves not to have years told.<br/><a name="Son-138-13"></a><span class="alignment" style="padding-left:10px;"> </span>Therefore I lie with her and she with me,<br/><a name="Son-138-14"></a><span class="alignment" style="padding-left:10px;"> </span>And in our faults by lies we flattered be.</div>
