<br/><a name="Son-025-01"></a>Let those who are in favor with their stars<br/><a name="Son-025-02"></a>Of public honor and proud titles boast,<br/><a name="Son-025-03"></a>Whilst I, whom fortune of such triumph bars,<br/><a name="Son-025-04"></a><span class="lineNbr" name="Son-025-04">4</span>Unlooked for joy in that I honor most.<br/><a name="Son-025-05"></a>Great princes’ favorites their fair leaves spread<br/><a name="Son-025-06"></a>But as the marigold at the sun’s eye,<br/><a name="Son-025-07"></a>And in themselves their pride lies burièd,<br/><a name="Son-025-08"></a><span class="lineNbr" name="Son-025-08">8</span>For at a frown they in their glory die.<br/><a name="Son-025-09"></a>The painful warrior famousèd for worth,<br/><a name="Son-025-10"></a>After a thousand victories once foiled,<br/><a name="Son-025-11"></a>Is from the book of honor razèd quite,<br/><a name="Son-025-12"></a><span class="lineNbr" name="Son-025-12">12</span>And all the rest forgot for which he toiled.<br/><a name="Son-025-13"></a><span class="alignment" style="padding-left:10px;"> </span>Then happy I, that love and am beloved<br/><a name="Son-025-14"></a><span class="alignment" style="padding-left:10px;"> </span>Where I may not remove nor be removed.</div>
